## App Description (EN-US)

**FairShift makes the invisible labor of household management visible, trackable, and fairly distributed.**

Unlike chore apps that only track who does what, FairShift reveals the hidden mental load — the thinking, planning, and remembering that keeps a home running.

Based on Eve Rodsky's Fair Play methodology, FairShift introduces the revolutionary three-layer ownership model:

🧠 **Conception** — Who thinks of it?
🚀 **Initiation** — Who starts it?
💪 **Execution** — Who does it?

**KEY FEATURES:**

🃏 **Fair Play Card Deck** — 80+ household responsibility cards. Swipe to assign. Each card represents a real task, from "Meal Planning" to "Remembering Birthdays."

⚖️ **Partnership Score** — See your fairness balance at a glance. Not about competition — it's about collaboration.

🧠 **Invisible Load Tracker** — First app to track mental labor alongside physical chores. "Remembering to buy toilet paper" counts too.

📊 **Pattern Radar** — Detects imbalances before they become arguments. "You carry 73% of the mental load" hits different than "You never help."

🔔 **Smart Nudge** — Non-judgmental reminders that respect Full Ownership. No nagging — just gentle awareness.

📸 **Retrospective Proof** — Log completions with photos. Zero management overhead.

**HOW IT WORKS:**

1. **Set up your partnership** — Add you and your partner
2. **Deal the cards** — Assign household responsibilities fairly
3. **Track completions** — Log what you do, including invisible work
4. **Check your balance** — See who carries more and where to shift
5. **Stay fair** — Pattern Radar keeps you accountable

**FREE vs PRO:**

Free: Basic task tracking, 10 Fair Play cards, single user
Pro: Full 80+ card deck, Mental Load Map, Pattern Radar, Smart Nudge, Partner Sync, and more

FairShift Pro: Monthly $3.99 | Yearly $24.99 | Lifetime $59.99
