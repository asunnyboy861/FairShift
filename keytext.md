## Description
FairShift makes the invisible labor of household management visible, trackable, and fairly distributed.

Unlike chore apps that only track who does what, FairShift reveals the hidden mental load - the thinking, planning, and remembering that keeps a home running.

Based on Eve Rodsky's Fair Play methodology, FairShift introduces the revolutionary three-layer ownership model:
- Conception - Who thinks of it?
- Initiation - Who starts it?
- Execution - Who does it?

KEY FEATURES:
- Fair Play Card Deck - 100+ household responsibility cards. Swipe to assign.
- Partnership Score - See your fairness balance at a glance.
- Invisible Load Tracker - First app to track mental labor alongside physical chores.
- Pattern Radar - Detects imbalances before they become arguments.
- Smart Nudge - Non-judgmental reminders that respect Full Ownership.
- Retrospective Proof - Log completions with photos.
- Partner Sync - Real-time sync between partners via CloudKit.
- Mental Load Map - Visualize who carries the cognitive burden.
