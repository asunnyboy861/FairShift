FairShift Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

TL;DR: FairShift collects almost no data. We don't track you, we don't sell your information, and your household data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

FairShift is designed with a privacy-first approach. We collect minimal data:

  • Household Data: Task assignments, completion logs, and partner information you enter. This data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • Account Data: If you subscribe to FairShift Pro, Apple processes your payment information. We never see your credit card details.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identifiers (email, phone number, address)
  • Location data
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Photos (proof photos are stored locally only)

2. How We Use Information

Your household data is used solely to provide the FairShift app functionality:

  • Displaying your task balance and fairness score
  • Generating Pattern Radar insights
  • Scheduling Smart Nudge reminders
  • Syncing between partners (if CloudKit is enabled)

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All household data is stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework with iOS encryption.
  • Cloud Sync: If you enable partner sync, data is transmitted via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure, encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • No Third-Party Servers: We do not operate any servers. Your data never passes through our infrastructure.

4. Third-Party Services

FairShift integrates only with Apple's first-party services:

  • StoreKit 2: For in-app purchases and subscription management. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • CloudKit: For optional partner sync. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • User Notifications: For Smart Nudge reminders. Notification content is generated locally.

We use zero third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Period.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your data exists only on your device (and your partner's device if synced).
  • Deleting the app removes all local data immediately.
  • CloudKit data can be managed in your iCloud settings.

7. Children's Privacy

FairShift is intended for adults managing household responsibilities. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

Since all data is stored locally, you can exercise these rights directly by managing or deleting the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
